Andrew the Gengar

Andrew is basically me but as a Gengar. He's a big nerd when it comes to technology, research, and gaming. However, he can be a bit lazy when it comes to school and tends to just lounge around in bed. He enjoys socializing but doesn’t start most conversations; instead, he prefers to join them.

Lenored Royle

Lenored is one of the first to meet Andrew and interacted with him the most. Nowadays, Lenored travels across the state, from forests to cities, talking to trainers about what they’ve encountered during their journeys.

Whimsicle the Haunter

From wild Pokémon to a goofy ghost, Whimsicle is a prankster like any normal Haunter. He’s also very agile. Lenored trained him to be a fighter when they first met, but Whimsicle still prefers pranks and jokes.

Brawno the Hypno

Brawno was born with a birth defect that made him unusually muscular! Though he’s a bit nervous and tends to stutter, Lenored helps him feel more confident. Brawno is even learning from Lenored’s research, despite his occasional fears.
